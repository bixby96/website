Spotify Anal-*YE*-sis

Introduction As the Internet of Things (IoT) landscape continues to grow, practically everything, it seems, is compiling data and generating statistics for any variable imaginable. And music is no exception. The music streaming service, Spotify, compiles an array of features for each song in its library. They record characteristics such as acousticness, danceability, energy and more. With these variables in mind, I conducted some exploratory analysis along with a couple clustering methods on Kanye West’s Spotify discography.
